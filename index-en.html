III. The founder - Portrait

Solenne Niedercorn-Desouches.

Solenne Niedercorn-Desouches
Founder

I'm not a journalist who covers finance. I come from finance.

Banker by training, twenty years at the crossroads of private banking, regulation and financial innovation. UBS, Lombard Odier, then advisory and boards - Kennet Partners, Fabric Ventures, Banque Wormser, Hokus.

In 2020, I founded Finscale with one conviction: European finance decision-makers deserve a space where technical depth and humanity coexist. Six years later: 350 bilingual episodes, 500,000 listens, and an audience brands cannot find anywhere else.

I am the insider speaking to insiders, in the two languages that matter in this ecosystem.

Format · 01

Branded Episode.

Your narrative carried in an in-depth conversation. 30 to 60 minutes. Full distribution: podcast + YouTube + newsletter.

Format · 02

Sponsor Pre-Roll.

Continuous presence across the full catalog, 1 to 3 months, tailored by language.

Format · 03

Private Talks.

10 to 12 senior decision-makers, invitation only. A single sponsor per event. Paris or Luxembourg.

Format · 04

Newsletter Sponsor.

Editorial visibility in the Sunday newsletter. Read by buy-side, banks, and European family offices.

Format · 05

Custom.

Exclusive briefings, branded editorials, annual packages. On request, in direct conversation.
